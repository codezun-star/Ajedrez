Chess piece SVGs — "Cburnett" standard set
===========================================

These 12 SVG files (wk, wq, wr, wb, wn, wp, bk, bq, br, bb, bn, bp) are the
standard chess pieces from Wikimedia Commons:
https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:SVG_chess_pieces/Standard

https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/

Authors:
  - User:Cburnett   — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User:Cburnett
  - User:Rfc1394    — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/User:Rfc1394

The individual files were extracted from the "standard" sprite bundled with
cm-chessboard (MIT, © Stefan Haack, https://github.com/shaack/cm-chessboard),
which itself adapted the Wikimedia Commons originals. No visual changes were
made beyond splitting the sprite into per-piece files.
